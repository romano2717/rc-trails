Who We Are

We are a group of RC hobbyists who enjoy exploring outdoor trails with our RC trucks. Beyond the hobby, we believe in building a strong community and giving back whenever we can.

Charity

Our outreach program focuses on helping families affected by the rising cost of living, especially due to the rapid increase in fuel prices. We aim to provide essential groceries to ease their daily burden.

Needed Donations

Preferred: Bank Transfer or GCash

To make the process easier for everyone, we highly encourage donations via bank transfer or GCash. This allows our team to handle purchasing and logistics efficiently, ensuring that all items are properly distributed to families in need.

Who Can Qualify for Grocery Donations

Donations are for one household only. Shared living situations are counted as one household (e.g., husband, wife, and a senior living together = 1 household).

Only households listed by the purok president will be eligible to receive donations.

Senior Citizens

Households that include senior citizens may qualify for assistance.

Single Parents

Single parents who are supporting their household on their own may qualify.

Persons with Disability

Households with a registered person with disability (PWD) may qualify.

Families with Kids

Families with children may qualify if:

• At least one parent currently has a job, OR
• If both parents are not currently employed, their last job must have been within the past 30 days.

Outreach Location

This outreach program will be held in Minglanilla, Pitogo Dos.
