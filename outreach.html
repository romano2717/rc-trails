Who We Are

We are a group of RC hobbyists who enjoy exploring outdoor trails with our RC trucks. Beyond the hobby, we believe in building a strong community and giving back whenever we can.

Charity

Our outreach program focuses on helping families affected by the rising cost of living, especially due to the rapid increase in fuel prices. We aim to provide essential groceries to ease their daily burden.

How to Help

We are looking for sponsors who are willing to support this initiative. Your contribution can help us reach more families and make a meaningful impact in the community.

Needed Donations

Preferred: Bank Transfer or GCash
