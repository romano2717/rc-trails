About the Outreach

Southside Cebu RC is organizing a community outreach program focused on helping families in need by providing essential groceries. With the increasing cost of living and rising fuel prices, many households are struggling with daily expenses.

Our Mission

To support families by providing basic food supplies and easing the burden caused by rising prices.

Who We Help

Families in the community who are affected by the increasing cost of living.

How You Can Help

Donate grocery items, share this initiative, or join us during the outreach activity.

Supporting Families in Difficult Times

Even small contributions can make a big difference. Together, we can help families get through these challenging times.

Needed Donations

You can help by donating any of the following grocery items:

Rice • Canned Goods • Instant Noodles • Coffee • Sugar • Milk • Cooking Oil • Biscuits

Outreach Location

This outreach program will be held in Minglanilla, Pitogo Dos.
