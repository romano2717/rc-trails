About the Outreach Program

Southside Cebu RC is organizing an outreach program focused on helping children by providing essential school supplies. Our goal is to support young students and encourage them as they continue their education.

Our Mission

To give kids the school supplies they need so they can start and continue learning with confidence.

Who We Help

Children in the community who can benefit from notebooks, pencils, bags, and other basic school materials.

How You Can Support

Join our outreach efforts by donating supplies, sharing the program, or volunteering during the event.

Giving Hope Through Education

Every notebook, pencil, and school item can make a real difference in a child’s school journey. Together, we can help build brighter futures.

Outreach Location

This outreach program will be held in a community in Minglanilla, Pitogo Dos.
